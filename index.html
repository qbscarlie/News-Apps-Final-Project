The growth of elementary schools in Columbia

By Carlie Procell
Published May 3, 2016
The amount of students enrolled in Columbia elementary schools has increased from 7975 to 8969 from 1992 to 2015. This has led to overcrowding in many elementary schools who are far above building capacity. Because of this, Columbia Public Schools closed a smaller school in 2009 (Eugene Field) and opened two larger schools in 2003 (Mary Paxton Keeley) and 2011 (Alpha Hart Lewis). But with Columbia expanding so rapidly, CPS is also opening an elementary school this fall and another one is currently under construction and scheduled to open in 2018.
